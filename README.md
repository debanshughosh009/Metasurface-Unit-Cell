The metasurfaces (MS) are planar periodical electrically thin structures and are used in the
management of electromagnetic waves. Their common uses are in suppression of mutual coupling,
increased bandwidth as well as radiation patterns of small arrays of antennas.

S-Parameter Behaviour

• S11 Resonance Near 5.15 GHz

As the strip width w is increased, the resonance moves to high frequencies.

• S21 Shows Weak Transmission (High Reflection)

The unit cell has a very high electric resonance, which behaves like a partially reflective
surface, which is an excellent property of metasurface superstrates.
